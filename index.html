UTD OVERWATCH CLUB

ABOUT US

Welcome to the UTD Overwatch Club. What started out as a small interest group became a strong, developed multitude of people. We are a community of people interested in playing Overwatch both casually and competitively. We welcome all skill levels, introduce scrimmages, and build relationships within the club. We founded this club because of the lack of non-competitive environment for Overwatch at UTD. We would love to have new members join and contribute to our growing population.
