Mission Statement

Welcome to the Overwatch club at UTD. What started out as a small interest group became a strong, developed multitude of people. We are a community of people interested in playing Overwatch both casually and competitively. We welcome all skill levels, introduce scrimmages, and build relationships within the club. We founded this club because of the lack of non-competitive environment for Overwatch at UTD. We would love to have new members join and contribute to our growing population.

Can I join?

Players of all skill level are encouraged to join! We offer active LFG channels, a club team, and a welcoming community.

Meetings

Meetings are every 3rd Friday at 3:00 PM. Plans may change due to campus policy, weather, or availability of our officers. If plans change, an announcement will be sent on the Discord server no later than that friday at 12:00 PM.
